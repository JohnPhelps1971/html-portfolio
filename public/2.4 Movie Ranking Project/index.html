The Best Movies According to John

My top 3 movies of all time

Apocalypse Now

The soundtrack and the atmosphere is simply amazing.

Edge of tomorrow

In terms of just never giving up, this is a great movie.
So much better than War of the Worlds, which is almost painful to watch with all the screaming.

Man on Fire

Fantastic movie - this is someone who takes their job seriously and gets things done. Dont mess with the nanny.
